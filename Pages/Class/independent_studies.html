Computer Science IV: Independent Studies is a class designated to be the last class a student studying computer science would take. As a result, the class is a lot looser than its prerequisites. Contrary to its name, the class is actually a series of labs and/or projects centered around the programming language chosen by the teacher. Such language usually changed every couple of months or so, based on the whims of the teacher. As a result, we were forced to experiment with new programming techniques and ways of thinking, as the same old solutions we knew were no longer viable to solve our new problems.
